Our Story

Maweja is more than just a cafe. It's a community hub where locals and visitors can enjoy freshly brewed coffee and discover incredible art from Malawian artists. Our mission is to celebrate local talent and create a welcoming space for creativity and connection. We believe that great coffee and great art go hand-in-hand, and we're dedicated to showcasing the best of both worlds right here in Lilongwe. Our journey began with a simple idea: to build a place where people could slow down, connect, and be inspired by the creativity that surrounds them.
